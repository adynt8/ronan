Accuracy Information

At PlsSize.Me, we are committed to providing the most accurate and reliable information possible. However, it's important to understand the limitations and potential sources of inaccuracy in our penis size calculator.

Sources of Potential Inaccuracy

  • Sample Size and Representation: While we use data from comprehensive studies, these still represent a limited sample of the global population.
  • Population Differences: We provide averages for different populations, but there can be significant individual variation within each group.
  • Limited Data on Certain Populations: Some populations may be underrepresented in scientific studies due to various factors.

Accuracy of Calculations

  • Percentile Calculations: We use the z-score method, assuming a normal distribution.
  • Volume and Surface Area Estimates: These assume a cylindrical shape, which is a simplification.
  • Condom Size Recommendations: Based on general guidelines, may not account for individual preferences.

Comparisons to Adult Film Actors

These measurements are often based on public claims or estimates and should be considered approximate.

Individual Variation and Personal Experience

Human bodies have a wide range of natural variation. Individual experiences may vary significantly.
